OUR STORY

TissBoda is the first of the app hailing services offered by Tiss Safaris Limited, that provides a safe, efficient and affordable way to “boda”.

Like many app hailing services, TissBoda comes in at a time when innovation and technology is providing the means to solve everyday problems. In line with the government’s Big 4 agenda to provide universal health coverage, TissBoda has come up with a solution that would provide the customer with an Added Value, from a deduction of 10% of the customer’s cost of riding towards their NHIF medical insurance cover in Real Time.
