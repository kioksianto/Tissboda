Phoebe Njoga

Chief Technical Officer

Passionate in technology and helping other people. Lending someone an extra hand, is and will always be the most important aspect of her life. Her interest to help others has driven her to volunteer in hospitals and homes for the old, a hospital and/or a health facility, a place that most shy away from. However, for Phoebe volunteering to help is much more enjoyable. Altruistically helping others satisfy's her than a paycheck. While volunteering in the various health facilities in Kenya, she noted that most Kenyans have not enrolled in any health insurance cover. Hence, the burden of paying the hospital bills becomes enormous and some of the people in hospitals are but are held in the hospital because of the pending bills. The question was how do we help these people? How do we empower the society now and in the future to realize that health insurance is very important? Fortunately, Kelly Lankeu was also thinking about empowering the society and had a brilliant idea that would not only meet the daily basic need of affordable transport, but also that would cater for individual and family health insurance and that's how I was brought on board. Phoebe also has a wealth of tax, regulatory and strategic management experience. She is responsible for outlining the company's technological vision, implementing technology strategies, and ensuring that the technological resources are aligned with the company's business needs.
