KELLY LANKEU

Founder and CEO

An alumnus of Kabarak University- School of Business specializing in Marketing and currently a post graduate student at Kenyatta University School of Business and Economics.In his line of duty, Lankeu worked briefly at the Social Health Insurance department, and thought of how BEST to leverage the gap of affordable health care to the old and vulnerable. Until when, he experienced a down turn moment, when one of his friends was diagnosed with a heart condition. Unbeknown to him, the friend didn’t have any kind of medical insurance, hence exposing him to danger. Lankeu connected the two incidences and this was how TissBoda came to be.This ride sharing application that provides a platform for making nudge Micro payments towards the National Health Insurance Scheme, came about to bridge the gap.
