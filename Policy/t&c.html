I. About TissBoda and Proper App Usage

TissBoda has through its software and app provides a platform through which Boda Boda and Tuk-tuk drivers are online and available for booking by riders. TissBoda unlike other transportation apps provides its users with not only an affordable and convenient means of transportation but also benefits its users (both the boda boda driver and rider) with its unique features to benefit its users with the opportunity to;

  • Enjoy part of the fees charged as a self-reward through a top up to the rider’s NHIF contributions. What this means is that TissBoda in partnership with the National Health Insurance Fund has enabled its riders to remit part of the riding fees towards the NHIF contribution. The rider will not be charged any additional fee for this service or any additional (booking) fees for the boda boda ride. TissBoda shall reserve a percentage of the riders fees that is due and payable into the riders NHIF account and upon subsequent rides and self rewards the rider shall be able to attain the minimum Kshs. 500/= required for remittance to NHIF for the Super Cover package. Upon attaining the required fee for contribution. TissBoda shall remit the amount to the riders NHIF account. TissBoda app provides transparency to its riders and its feature enables the rider to view its NHIF top up dummy statement after every ride. TissBoda retains the right to subject a charge at any given time upon the company’s discretion. Charges may or may not be imposed on the user at a later stage.
  • The rider enjoys the benefit of an array of payment options which includes; cash, wallet or Visa Card.
  • TissBoda has through its features enabled riders to share and top up the amount in the riders wallet. What this means is that a rider can send and receive cash in the wallet provided in the app. This will enable the user to conveniently pay for individual transport fees or pay for a third-party user of the TissBoda app.
  • The rider shall enjoy the benefit of e-GiftVoucher once the rider attains the limit of the NHIF contributions. What this means is that TissBoda shall have remitted the rider’s NHIF contribution and any subsequent self-rewards towards NHIF contributions after each ride will create a surplus which TissBoda shall reward as an e-Giftvoucher. The rider shall receive the digital voucher via SMS and shall redeem the e-voucher at any supermarket/ store or any merchant receiving payment via Safaricom lipa na mpesa buygoods till.
  • TissBoda in partnership with the County Pension Fund provides its boda boda drivers with the advantage to benefit from the products of CPF. The use of the app by boda boda drivers will benefit the drivers towards a contribution of their pension fund, Life Insurance Cover and Medical cover. TissBoda considers the risks and exposure involved by boda boda riders and has taken steps to ease the lives of drivers. TissBoda shall purpose to update and improve its services through its partnerships for the benefit of drivers.
