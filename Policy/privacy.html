OUR PRIVACY POLICY

I.Introduction

Your privacy is important to TissBoda. TissBoda ride shall therefore take all reasonable steps to protect the personal data of users. For the purpose of this clause “personal data” shall be defined as detailed in the Data Protection Act No. 24 of 2019. The Data Protection Act may be downloaded from: http://kenyalaw.org:8181/exist/kenyalex/actview.xql?actid=No.%2024%20of%202019. TissBoda has developed a privacy statement that covers how we collect, use and disclose, transfer and store your information. Please take a moment to familiarize yourself with our policy.

II. Applicability

The Privacy Policy applies to all users of TissBoda Services including users of the app and website or any other services offered by TissBoda. This Notice specifically applies to;

  • Riders: Any person who requests or receives transportation, including those who receive transportation requested by another individual
  • Boda Boda Drivers: Any person who provides transportation to riders.

All those subject to this policy are referred to as ‘users’ in this policy.

Our data practices are subject to the applicable laws in place in which we operate. This means that the App is governed by the Laws of Kenya and TissBoda chooses as its domicillium Citendi et executandi for all purposes under this agreement. Whether in respect of court processes, notice of other documents or communication of whatsoever nature.

III. Data Collection and Uses

  • Information Requested: All information requested by TissBoda Software upon the users request for registration is requested in order to offer you our professional services (for taxi and parcel services) as well as the app specific feature for NHIF and Pension Fund contribution. The personal data requested and submitted voluntarily shall be processed in accordance with the users right to privacy. The users personal data shall be processed lawfully, fairly and in a transparent manner. When the user downloads the TissBoda App, we collect your device type, your wireless carrier and the users Individual device ID which is associated with your personal data all to primarily support your account and provide the user with TissBoda Services.
  • User Account:The user is responsible for maintaining confidentiality of your password and account and is fully accountable for all activities under the users account. You may allow other members of your household to use the app under your account and the user agrees to pay all charges that shall accrue in respect to such usage. The user also agrees to supervise all usage of the app by minors.
  • Information collected:TissBoda shall collect tracking information of the user whenever the App of software is in use. Whenever the user is using the app, the phone of the boda boda driver shall record the riders GPS coordinates. This information is solely collected to provide the user with the history of the rides. TissBoda also collects the device type and unique identifier when its application is in use. This information is used for the sole purpose of providing the user with the most updated application and features. The information of the rider’s geo-location is also solely tracked to enable you view the boda boda drivers close to you for the purpose of using the app. TissBoda may collect the users geo-location when the app is not in use for the purpose of providing its users with efficient geo-based services however such use shall require the users consent who may terminate such usage from the individual device at any time.
  • Usage:Our primary goal as TissBoda in collecting information is to provide the user with an enhanced experience in our services. TissBoda uses the collected information for various purposes as outlined below; i. To welcome the user to the TissBoda App or Website upon registration ii. To closely monitor which features of TissBoda services are popular with the users. iii. To allow the user to view the trip history iv. To use the user’s personal data for the purpose of submitting individual NHIF and Pension Contributions to the specific authorities. v. To inform the user of any promotions running by TissBoda vi. To enable TissBoda determine which features it needs to improve, including usage patterns and geographic locations and resources. vii. To provide communication to the user during pick up and drop off. viii. To provide the user with the correct version of the app depending on the user’s device type. ix. For troubleshooting and in some cases for marketing purposes.

NB: Generally, the user has an option to opt-out of receiving certain communications from TissBoda such as TissBoda newsletter by clicking on the ‘Unsubscribe’ link at the bottom of each email or by emailing TissBoda at info@tissboda.com . Please note that even when the user unsubscribes or opts-out, TissBoda may still send the users boda boda ride related bookings communication.

Cookies and other anonymous information:

When the TissBoda software is in use, TissBoda collect anonymous Information such as the user’s IP address and standard web log information, such as the browser type and the pages accessed on our website, browser language, the date and time of the request and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify the user’s browser. TissBoda may track the pages that directed the user to TissBoda software, though we do not associate that information with the user’s personal information. TissBoda may also place small data files called “cookies” on the users’s computer or mobile device. Cookies do not harm or damage the user’s system in any way. However, it makes the user’s navigation easier as it save the settings. TissBoda will send a “session cookie” to the user’s computer or mobile device when you log in to your account. This type of cookie helps TissBoda to recognize the user when you visit multiple areas on our site during the same session, so that we don’t need to ask you for your password on each page. Once the user logs out, close the browser or application, this cookie expires and it no longer has any effect. We also use longer-lasting cookies to display the users e-mail address or mobile phone number on TissBoda sign-in form, so that the user dosent need to retype the e-mail address each time the account is in use.The user is free to decline TissBoda cookies if the user browser permits, but doing so may interfere with your use of our software. TissBoda may use third party tracking services to track and analyse anonymous information to users of our software. Such third parties may use cookies to help track user behavior. We reserve the right to disclose anonymous information publicly without restriction, including to third party vendors who may help us enhance or provide the software or TissBoda services or to third parties.
